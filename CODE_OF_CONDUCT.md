# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in the
BOSC Community Library a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of
age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, experience level,
nationality, religion, or sexual identity.

## Our Standards
**Positive behavior includes:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Showing empathy toward other community members

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Harassment, trolling, or insulting comments
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement
Violations may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be
reviewed and investigated. Maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality.
