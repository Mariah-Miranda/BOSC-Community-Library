# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in BOSC Community Library a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, experience level, nationality, religion, or sexual identity.

## Our Standards

Positive behavior includes:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback with care.
- Showing empathy toward other community members.
- Protecting the dignity of students, educators, librarians, and public servants who use the project.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, trolling, intimidation, or insulting comments.
- Publishing another person's private information without consent.
- Dismissing contributors on the basis of background, language, institution, or technical experience.
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional public-sector setting.

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to the project maintainers. Maintainers will review complaints promptly, maintain confidentiality where possible, and take proportionate action to protect the community.
